📜 Historical Map Collection – Scenario Pack

Rediscover the great eras of modern history through a series of immersive scenarios, each offering unique maps, balances, and dynamics.

Each module of the Historical Map Mod places you in a key historical period, with nations, technologies, and tensions adapted to their historical context — while leaving room for major divergences.

⚔️ Napoleonic Wars (1804 – 1814)

Europe is dominated by a single ambition: that of Napoléon Bonaparte.
Between successive coalitions, total wars, and political upheaval, every decision can reshape the balance of the continent.

- Start your game in 1804 with a dominant French Empire across continental Europe
- Expanding empires or resisting powers
- Complete reorganization of Europe

👑 The Victorian Era (1837 – 1901)

Under the reign of Queen Victoria, the world enters an era of industrialization and social transformation.

- Start your game at the coronation of Her Majesty Queen Victoria in 1837
- Alternative map compared to the vanilla game for greater depth and detail
- Industrial and technological explosion
- Massive colonial expansion
- Rise of modern great powers

🔥 Civil War (1861 – 1866)

The United States is torn apart by war between the Union and the Confederacy, a decisive conflict for the nation’s future.

- Start your game at the outbreak of the American division in 1861
- Large-scale industrial warfare
- Massive mobilization and war economy
- An uncertain outcome that can reshape global balance

🌍 The Scramble for Africa (1880 – 1900)

European powers engage in a fierce race for control over Africa.

- Start your game after the Franco-Prussian War in 1871, at the dawn of the African partition
- Rapid colonization and imperial rivalries
- Constantly shifting borders
- Diplomatic tensions that may escalate into major conflicts

✨ La Belle Époque (1890 – 1914)

A period of prosperity, innovation, and optimism… on the surface.

- Start your game in the heart of the Belle Époque in 1890
- Major technological advancements
- Growing arms race
- Fragile alliances leading toward global war

⚖️ Interwar Period (1919 – 1939)

After World War I, the world attempts to rebuild, but tensions remain high.

- Start your game in the aftermath of World War I in 1919
- New states and unstable borders
- Economic crises and rising ideologies
- Toward an inevitable new global conflict
- Can be enhanced with mods such as Tech & Res
